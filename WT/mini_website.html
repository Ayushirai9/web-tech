About GL Bajaj

GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management is a premier engineering college located in Greater Noida, India. It offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and management.

The institute is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong emphasis on research and innovation.

GL Bajaj is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management (GLBITM) is a private engineering and management college located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh. It's known as one of the top engineering and management institutions in North India and Uttar Pradesh. The college is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow.
